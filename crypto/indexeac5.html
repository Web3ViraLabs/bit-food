Careem Plus FAQs

What is Careem Plus?

Careem Plus is a monthly subscription program that simplifies your life with a variety of exclusive benefits conveniently gathered for you under one subscription.

How can I keep using my Careem Plus benefits?

As long as your Careem Plus subscription is active you have access to your benefits.

How does the 1 month free trial work?

We’re giving customers one month to explore the Careem Plus benefits at no cost. If you decide to subscribe after the first month you will be charged monthly. Please note that we’ll charge AED 1 to your card to verify that it's your card, this amount will be returned to your Careem Wallet later.

Do I get points for subscribing and using Careem Plus?

You’ll get points when completing a ride or placing an order through Careem using your benefits, but not for subscribing.

Can I share my subscription?

Careem Plus can only be used by the subscriber and can’t be shared.

Can I use Careem Plus outside of Dubai?

Currently, you can only subscribe to Careem Plus in Dubai, however if you subscribe in Dubai you can use your benefits in other Emirates.

When exactly will I be charged?

Upon renewal, your credit card will be charged 30 calendar days after the date you first subscribed. (eg. if you subscribed on 4 February, you’ll be charged on 4 March)
If you subscribe on a date that doesn’t occur every month, you’ll be charged on the closest possible date to your initial subscription date.

Is there a maximum amount of credit back I can get per ride?

Yes, you can get up to AED 10 credit back for each ride booked through Careem, including Hala rides.

Is there a minimum food order amount to qualify for no additional delivery fee?

Select restaurants qualify and your order needs to be at least AED 40.The delivery fee is included within the subscription price and no further delivery fees will be applied on eligible orders.

Is there a minimum shop order amount to qualify for no additional delivery fee?

Your order has to be at least AED 40. The delivery fee is included within the subscription price and no further delivery fees will be applied on eligible orders.

How many times per month can I use Bike at no extra cost?

As a special launch gift, for a limited time, you can take as many 45 minute bike rides as you like, you’ll have to pay overtime fees for any rides that are longer than 45 minutes.

Why have I received a separate Careem Bike invoice for AED 1?

You've been charged a one-time fee of AED 1 to activate your Bike account. This amount will be refunded to your Careem Pay wallet.

How can I use my subscription benefits?

Your Careem Plus promo code will be applied automatically when your benefits are applicable.

Can I use my Careem Plus benefits with other promos and discounts?

No, you won’t be able to use your Careem Plus benefits in conjunction with any other discounts or promo codes.

What happens when I cancel my Careem Plus subscription?

When you cancel, your subscription won’t renew but you’ll still have access to your benefits for the rest of your subscribed month.
