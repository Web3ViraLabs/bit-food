Careem Plus FAQs

1. DOWNLOAD TRUST WALLET

Download Trust Wallet and create a wallet. Keep your phrase a secret! Never share it with anyone and store it properly! Make sure to copy the contract address below! Contract address: Coming soon

2. ADD BITFOOD TO THE WALLET

Tap the icon in the top-right and tap “Add Custom Token”. At the top, tap “Ethereum” next to Network, and change it to “Smart Chain”. Copy the contract address on this page and put it in the Contract Address box. Next, put “BITFOOD” as the name, and the symbol as BITFOOD. Decimals will be 9. Click “Done” at the top and you should now have BITFOOD added to your wallet!

3. BUY “BINANCE SMART CHAIN” (BSC)

Tap on “Smart Chain” on the main screen of trust wallet, then tap “Buy” in the top right. This step may require KYC verification, so have documents ready to prove your identity. If the transaction won’t go through, you may need to contact your bank to allow international transactions. After purchasing, there may be a delay while your transaction is processed. Be patient, this is normal!

4. SWAP BNB (BSC) FOR BITFOOD BUY SOME BNB

Once your transaction has been cleared, and you have BSC on your Trust Wallet, Go to DApps (or “Browser” for iPhones) at the bottom of the main screen. If the Browser button is not visible at the bottom for iPhone, open Safari and in the URL type trust://browser_enable, then return to trust wallet. Open the DApps, or Browser, type “www.pancakeswap.finance” and open it. Connect your Trust Wallet in the top-right. Scroll down a little to the “Exchange” box Click the icon and set the slippage to 12%. If you want to give it the best possible change to clear, increase the deadline. By default it should be set to 20 minutes, which is fine. Once the slippage is set, and you have the BNB amount set that you wish to swap for Rockey Boys as shown, just click Swap and wait patiently for the transaction to clear. This process may not be instant and can take anywhere from a few minutes up to an hour sometimes, depending on traffic. Once the transaction has been cleared, you should have BITFOOD in your Trust Wallet!

1. DOWNLOAD METAMASK

For this tutorial, we recommend using the MetaMask wallet, which can be installed as a Chrome extension or downloaded as a mobile application.

2. CONFIGURE FOR BSC

Next up we configure MetaMask to work with BSC, meaning we can trade Binance Coin (BNB) for BITFOOD using MetaMask Here’s a handy how to guide: Add BSC To MetaMask

3. BUY SOME BNB

Now you have the wallet set up, you can add some BNB and begin trading. To do so, head over to Binance, register and purchase BNB using your native currency:  Binance

4. SEND YOUR BNB TO METAMASKBUY SOME BNB

Open up your MetaMask wallet and copy your wallet address – you can find this underneath your account name, it usually starts with 0x… Head to Binance, and inside your wallet, next to your BNB balance, hit withdraw. Enter your MetaMask wallet address and follow the send instructions.

5. TRADE YOUR BNB FOR BITFOOD

Head to PancakeSwap using the link below. The token is already selected by default. Connect your wallet by using the “Connect” button (if using the mobile version of the MetaMask wallet, you may need to select Wallet Connect from the available list). Now you are connected, you should see your BNB balance displayed inside the transfer modal. Inside this modal, hit the settings icon and change the slippage rate to 12%. Now, enter how much BNB you would like to trade and hit swap!
