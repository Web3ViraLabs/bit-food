About us.

Our Story

BitFood is the internet platform for Qatar and greater to expand across the Middle East region. A pioneer of the region's crypto revolutionized economy, BitFood is expanding services across its platform to include crypto trading, P2P transaction and delivery of goods to become the region's everyday SuperApp. BitFood's mission is to simplify and improve the lives of people and build a lasting organization that inspires. BitFood was established in July 2021 while the idea was first presented in November 2020, ever since the work was nonstop to ensure we can provide the best possible application. BitFood is set to operate in over 100 cities by the end of 2022 and expand to over 15 countries. Through this process of expansion, we are expecting to create more than one million employment opportunities in the region.
